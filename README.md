GAP YEAR MANIFESTATION CARD 

###what is this?
this is my first ever PCB design and i named it Gap Year Manifestation Card.
-------------
###what does it do?
the front side has my instagram QR code, an NFC antenna, an LED light and a resistor. when you tap the card directly to any phone, it scans and my instagram profile opens directly on the screen. no app needed, just tap.

-----------------

###why did i make this?
i'm on a gap year right now and this is literally my first month of it.
being from indian society, taking a gap year is like breaking all the norms here.
i'm going in a completely different direction. i want to feel that confidence — that what i'm learning is real, what i'm doing is real, and these skills actually exist. this project is proof of that. it's really going to help me believe in myself more
