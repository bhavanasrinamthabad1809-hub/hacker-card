# Gap Year Manifestation Card 🌟

## What is this?

This is my first ever PCB design — a **Gap Year Manifestation Card**.

It started as a simple idea: make an NFC business card with my Instagram QR code 
and phone number. But as I kept working on it, something shifted in me. I'm currently 
on a gap year, and I wanted this card to mean something more. So I turned it into a 
**Gap Year Manifestation Card** — a physical reminder of this chapter of my life, 
the risks I'm taking, and the person I'm becoming.

## My Journey

When I started this, I was someone who had **zero idea** how PCB design works.
I didn't know what a Gerber file was. I didn't know how EasyEDA worked.
I didn't know how to lay out a board, route traces, or export anything.

But I figured it out — one small thing at a time.
Every confused moment, every YouTube rabbit hole, every "wait that actually worked" —
it all added up. And now I have a real, manufacturable PCB design that I made myself.

That feeling? Incredibly worth it. 🙌
