
# Gap Year Manifestation Card 🌟

## what is this?
this is my first ever PCB design and i named it Gap Year Manifestation Card.

## what does it do?
the front side has my instagram QR code, an NFC antenna, an LED light and a resistor. when you tap the card directly to any phone, it scans and my instagram profile opens directly on the screen. no app needed, just tap.

## why did i make this?
i'm on a gap year right now and this is literally my first month of it.
being from indian society, taking a gap year is like breaking all the norms here. i'm going in a completely different direction. i want to feel that confidence — that what i'm learning is real, what i'm doing is real, and these skills actually exist. this project is proof of that. it's really going to help me believe in myself more.

## how to use it
i am going to meet so many people this year because i am on a gap year. when i go for meetings i just tap the card on their phone at my instagram box and my profile opens instantly. i have everything on my instagram — linkedin, resume, my work as a video editor and graphic designer. when i share that, i can get freelancing work, build a network, make money from it. as far as my identity goes with that card, i grow that much, i can build that much. that's what this card means to me.

## how to build it
i designed this in EasyEDA — it's free and works in the browser. here's what i used:

- **NTAG213** — the NFC chip (this is what makes the tap work)
- **LED** — indicator light
- **Resistor** — to protect the LED
- **PCB** — i designed it myself in EasyEDA, drew all my doodles on the bottom silk layer, added the NFC antenna coil, placed all components and routed the connections
- export gerber files from EasyEDA → order from JLCPCB → solder the components onto the pads → done!
